Introduction

Climate change relates to major changes in the temperature, precipitation, and others of the atmosphere for many years. This is due to fossil fuel combustion and deforestation, which brings about industrial processes mainly associated with human activities that induce global warming, increase in sea levels, extreme climate conditions, and a disruption in the ecosystem. The need is for adequate global cooperation, innovation in sources of renewable energy, and appropriate practices that reduce the greenhouse emissions and address its impacts.
